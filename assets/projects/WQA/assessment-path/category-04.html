Helpful Information

This is the Efficiency page! Efficiency is based off how well a website does what it should do. You want to make sure that the user is feeling that the website is coherent, sequential and logical. If you have an eCommerce site, you want to make sure your customers see a sequential and logical checkout process. If it is a blog site, a simple way to respond to users will want users to interact with your website. Most importantly, you want to make sure your website is accessible to all users and supports users with disabilities. Here you will have the option to grade your site from “Strongly Disagree” to “Strongly Agree” or you can keep the selection as N/A (Not Available). Once you have graded the efficiency of your website, you may click next to continue on assessing your website or click back to make changes on the previous assessment pages.
