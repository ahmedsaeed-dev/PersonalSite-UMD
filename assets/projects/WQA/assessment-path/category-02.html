Helpful Information

This is the Functionality page! Functionality is based off the ease of which a user can navigate your website and obtain the information they are seeking. It is also based off if your website works for various browsers and mobile devices, the design of the website is consistent such as it is visually appealing, and it lets the user know which page they are currently on. Here you will have the option to grade your site from “Strongly Disagree” to “Strongly Agree” or you can keep the selection as N/A (Not Available). Once you have graded the functionality of your website, you may click next to continue on assessing your website or click back to make changes on the Usability page.
