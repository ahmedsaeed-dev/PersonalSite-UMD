Helpful Information

This is the Reliability page! Reliability is based off various factors that the user will want to check on before continuing to use the website. You will want to make sure your website is clear on the information it will be providing, the images and links provided are working, the data presented could be reloaded without any bugs occurring, and the information on the site is up-to-date. Here you will have the option to grade your site from “Strongly Disagree” to “Strongly Agree” or you can keep the selection as N/A (Not Available). Once you have graded the reliability of your website, you may click next to continue on assessing your website or click back to make changes on the previous assessment pages.
