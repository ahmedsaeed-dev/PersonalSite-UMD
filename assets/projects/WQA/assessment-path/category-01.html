Helpful Information

This is the Usability page! Usability is based off the ease of use of your website. The goals are how presentable your information and choices are to the user. This will make sure your choices are clear and in a concise way that any user will be able to understand. It will also make sure that the placement of important items are in appropriate areas. Here you will have the option to grade your site from “Strongly Disagree” to “Strongly Agree” or you can keep the selection as N/A (Not Available). Once you have graded the usability of your website, you may click next to continue on assessing your website or click back to the Pre-Assessment page.
